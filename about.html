About our school

Krioukov Academy is one of the projects of the School of Fine Art and Design in Berlin. This school is a private school of continuing education founded in 2003 by a modern painter Andrei Krioukov. The School offers various courses for both children of all ages making the first steps in drawing and also for those students who are going to become later professionals and who are looking for the sources of new art techniques and inspiration. The graduates of the School work successfully as independent painters and teach art disciplines.

School of Fine Art and Design

The School of Fine Arts and Design was founded by the painter Andrei Krioukov in Kassel in 2003. In 2009 the school moved to Berlin, Prenzlauer Berg. Our school is a private supplementary school according to ยง 102 SchulG Berlin and is AZWV certified. We offer professional, certified training in painting, stage design, illustration, comics, animation drawing and digital art, as well as in art and culture management. Students who have completed a course of study in drawing/painting or illustration with us work as freelancers, teach art courses, do commissioned work, or continue their studies in this direction at colleges and universities. Our team

The lecturers at the School of Fine Arts and Design are academically trained artists who use their artistic know-how to teach in an exciting and professionally sound manner in their field. Through their many years of experience in the art business, they have a sharpened critical awareness of the decisions and course settings that are important for the SBKG students in order to successfully embark on the path to professional life as artists.

Graduates

More than 50 students graduated our art school. After graduating they either become free painters, illustrators and other specialists or they decide to improve their skills by entering an art college. Our graduates are well prepared for their further paths in the artistic world whatever they choose to do after school.
